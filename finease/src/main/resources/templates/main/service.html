Our Services

We focus solely on enhancing financial accessibility for individuals with visual impairments, aligning our services with our project's objectives and scope. Ensuring our solutions are tailored to meet your needs effectively.

Text To Speech

Integration of a TTS engine into the online financial platform to convert on-screen text into speech for users with visual impairments.

PDF Accessibility

Integration of TTS technology to enable visually impaired users to access and comprehend financial documents in PDF format.

Navigation Aid

Development of a navigation aid feature that provides real-time auditory descriptions of interactive components on the website.
